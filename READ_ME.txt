### Targeted Queue Generator
Creates csv output of a WCMC targeted queue with injection names, well assignments, and index for reordering into injection order or client list order. 

####Prerequisites
- Python 3.7
- pandas 0.24.2

####Inputs
Filepath to client sample list (Excel sheet). (Shift-right click -> "Copy as path")
Minix number
Platform (Bile Acids, Steroids, or Oxylipins)
Randomization


Will save output to the directory of the input file.
